FAQs

Am I eligible?

Our Startup Program is open to organizations with less than $3M in total funding and who have been in business under 3 years.

What happens next?

The Orbit team will get back to you in one working day via the email you submitted.

If you're accepted, your Orbit account will be good to go! We'll send an invite to the Startup Community in Discord and a link to meet with your Customer Success Manager.

If you don't quite meet the criteria, then we'll help guide you towards the offering that works best for you.

What happens after?

At the end of the year, you can always switch to our free plan, or continue at a discounted rate.
